FAQ

  1. Can I bring friends? Yes, please! But if you bring lots of friends, consider bringing some extra drinks to offset! If any of your friends have already been blocklisted from my parties, please do not bring them.
  2. Will you be providing food? It's important to eat while drinking, so yes, we will have some snacks and desserts! I recommend eating a meal before coming.
  3. What's your policy on sleepovers? I have a zero-tolerance policy against drinking and driving (one way to get on the blocklist), so if you need a safe place to stay, I will always provide, no questions asked. That being said, please be aware that if 20 people need to stay over, the accommodations won't be very comfy, so try to have a safe ride plan.
  4. What are your other zero-tolerance rules? #1 Harrassment. I love when people meet each other through my parties, it's a big reason that I throw them, but consent is non-negotiable, and comfort should always be a top-priority. If you feel like someone is/was being inappropriate, feel free to let me know, and I'll handle the situation. #2 Disrespect. Like I mentioned, this is not an event space, so I expect everyone to be respectful of my neighbors out in the hallway when coming and going. If the neighbors have a problem with my parties, I can't throw them! #3 Just generally being an ass, don't do that.
  5. What is your blocklist? I've thrown quite a few parties over the years, and there are a few people that have broken my zero tolerance rules and were asked not to come back. They should know.
